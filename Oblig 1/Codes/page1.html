The history of Ninjago

Long before time had a name, the First Spinjitzu master created Ninjago using four elemental weapons. But when he passed, a dark presence sought out to collect them all. So I, Sensei Wu, his brother, sought out to find four ninja to collect them first. Sign up to join the journey.

Some random information

The 6 ninjas are Lloyd (green ninja), Kai (fire ninja), Jay (lightning ninja), Zane (ice ninja), Nya (water ninja) and Cole (earth ninja)

Each elemental weapon will correspond to one of the original ninjas elemental powers. The weapons are Shurikens of ice, Scythe of quakes, Sword of fire and Nunchucks of lightning.
